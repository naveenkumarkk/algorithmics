What is the AI Tutor?

The AI Tutor is designed to guide you through all stages of the Anticipation-Action-Reflection (AAR) cycle, making learning more effective and personalized. AI tutors should guide you to reflect on your individual learning processes rather than providing ready-made solutions. This approach ensures that you will engage thoroughly in the forethought phase, which is essential for effective self-regulation.

What are the benefits of using an AI Tutor in the learning process?

The AI Tutor enhances learning by supporting Self-Regulated Learning (SRL), helping you plan, monitor, and reflect effectively. It aligns with the AAR cycle (Anticipation, Action, Reflection) to develop skills like responsibility, focus, and adaptability. Here’s how the AI Tutor benefits you:

1. Plan Smarter:
Set clear goals and schedules with strategic planning tools.

2. Stay on Track:
Use self-monitoring strategies for feedback and adjustments during tasks.

3. Learn from Reflection:
Link actions to outcomes, identifying successes and areas for improvement.

How does the Planning Phase work?

In the Planning Phase, the AI Tutor encourages you to create your own plan, helping you build essential self-regulation skills. At the start, it helps you set clear goals and deadlines, emphasizing the importance of taking an active role in your learning. Instead of giving you a ready-made plan, the tutor uses strategic planning techniques to support you, such as:

1. Asking questions to help you think about the main steps, available time, and your goals.

2. Offering tips, like breaking tasks into smaller, manageable parts.

3. Using reflective prompts, such as “What is the first action you'll take to get started?”

This approach ensures you stay in control of your plan, with the AI Tutor offering support along the way. Once your plan is ready, the Monitoring Phase begins.

How does the Monitoring Phase work?

In the Monitoring Phase, the AI Tutor focuses on boosting your internal motivation rather than relying on external reminders to keep you on track. The goal is to help you actively monitor your progress and make adjustments as needed, encouraging you to strengthen your self-regulatory skills and build effective learning habits. The tutor guides you through:

Progress Check-ins: Encouraging you to assess how you're doing and if you're staying on track with your goals and timeline.

Adjustment Support: If you're falling behind or encountering challenges, the AI Tutor helps you explore ways to improve your plan and progress.

Once you've completed the task, the monitoring phase ends, and the next phase—Reflecting—begins, where you evaluate your performance and identify areas for improvement.

How does the Reflecting Phase work?

In the Reflecting Phase, after completing your tasks, the AI Tutor helps you evaluate your performance. This phase encourages you to identify what worked well and what didn’t, fostering self-awareness and providing insights for improvement. The reflection process is based on causal attribution, where you analyze the connection between your actions (causes) and their outcomes (effects). By creating a cause-effect pattern in your mind, you can better understand what led to your success or challenges and identify potential pitfalls to avoid in the future. This helps you make adjustments and continuously improve your learning process.
